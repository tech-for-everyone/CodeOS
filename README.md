# DevOS
## ATTENCION DEVOS .ISO PRODUCTION HAS BEEN STOPPED TEMPORARLY
DevOS is a modern operating system built for developers who live on the cutting edge and want a fast, minimal, and highly customizable environment. From the kernel up, DevOS is designed to make experimentation easy, integrate powerful tools, and get out of your way so you can focus on building what’s next.

Whether you’re trying out new languages, pushing hardware to its limits, or rapidly prototyping ambitious ideas, DevOS gives you a lean, developer-first foundation that stays lightweight while giving you full control.

# Features
Developer-First Design – Built from the ground up for programmers, with sensible defaults, minimal bloat, and a focus on getting real work done.

Highly Customizable Environment – Shape everything to your workflow: the shell, window manager, and even low-level system behavior are all fair game.

Optimized for Performance – Lean system services, fast boot times, and low overhead mean more of your hardware is available for builds, tests, and experiments.

Advanced Tooling Integration – First-class support for modern languages, debuggers, profilers, and build systems right out of the box.

Experimentation-Friendly – Easily spin up, isolate, and tear down experimental setups without cluttering or breaking your main environment.

Hardware-Tinker Ready – Great for stress-testing devices, benchmarking, and digging into low-level system behavior.

Evolving Platform – Rapid iteration and frequent updates, driven by the needs of developers working on the bleeding edge.

# Status
DevOS is still in an early stage of development. Expect rapid changes, frequent updates, and the occasional breaking change as the project evolves.

# Getting Started
Right now, DevOS is experimental and aimed at advanced users who are comfortable working with low-level systems and aren’t afraid to debug or tinker.

# Check Requirements
Before you dive in, make sure you have compatible hardware and can dedicate a test machine or virtual machine. DevOS is not yet recommended as a daily driver.
